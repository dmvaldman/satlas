Account Deletion Instructions

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your Satlas account, you can do so directly within the app.

How to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the Satlas app and sign in to your account.
  2. Tap on your profile icon or the menu button to open your profile settings.
  3. In the profile settings screen, tap the "Delete Account" button.
  4. A confirmation prompt will appear. Please read it carefully, as this action is irreversible.
  5. Confirm the deletion. Your account and all associated data, including your sits and photos, will be permanently deleted.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your user profile (username, email, etc.).
  • All "sits" you have created.
  • All photos you have uploaded.
  • Any other marks or contributions you have made.

This action cannot be undone. If you decide to use Satlas again in the future, you will need to create a new account.
